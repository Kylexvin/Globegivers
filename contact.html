How to Volunteer

Volunteering with GlobeGivers is a rewarding way to make a difference in the world. Here’s how you can get involved:

  • Step 1: Choose a Program - Select a program that aligns with your skills and interests.
  • Step 2: Apply - Send an email to volunteer@globegivers.com with your name, contact information, and the program you are interested in.
  • Step 3: Interview - We will contact you to discuss your application and how you can contribute.
  • Step 4: Training - Attend a training session to prepare for your role.
  • Step 5: Get Started - Begin making a positive impact in your chosen program.
